About BloodLine


Blood transfusion safety remains an important public health concern in Pune. The availability of blood products of all blood types and the provision of its safety ensure public trust of its excellent healthcare system.

However, lack of availability of these blood products and provision of unsafe blood products still impact morbidity and mortality in the city. Through the use of online blood bank management system, blood transfusion safety is expected to be improved. Risks of Donor Information manhandling can be avoided. Also, processes involving blood bag collection, storage, and inventory will be systematized and organized, hence, improving the blood provision & management.

We at BloodLine strive to:

  • 1.To ensure hospital to have rich supply or inventories of blood bags.
  • 2.To check the availability of blood bags anytime.
  • 3.To manage the information of its blood donor.
  • 4.To allow good documentation about the donor and its blood donation activities.
  • 5.Support fast searching.

The main aim of BloodLine is therefore to establish a forum for people connected to potential blood banks as well as blood donors throughout the City.


From The Creator's Desk

"I was in a Hospital paying visit to my friend who was ill and had been admitted in the Hospital. While i was on my way Home after meeting my friend, a woman at the door of the hospital stopped me enquiring about my blood group. She was in a lot of tension, She urgently needed someone who could donate AB Negative blood to her son who had suffered injuries in a bike accident. The blood stock was nowhere to be available near the hospital that we were in. It was then, that the very idea of creating BloodLine struck my mind. An Online Portal that encourages people to donate blood and ensures that the blood stock of any type doesn't get exhausted throughout the city."

Atharva Deshmukh

(Co-Creator: "BloodLine")

"Atharva came up to me with this idea of creating BloodLine and I thought this initiative would be of great help. India is a densely populated country and on an average 414 accidents take place every day, More than 38,000 blood donations are needed every day. A total of 30 million blood components are transfused each year. This is a huge number which can only be fulfilled if each and everyone of us contribute towards saving a life. Our aim is to spread awareness about the same through our website and supply blood to the needy."

Soham Bhirangi

(Co-Creator: "BloodLine")
