National Voluntary Blood Donation Day

On 1 October every year National Voluntary Blood Donation Day is observed in India since 1975 to encourage healthy people to donate blood. It also seeks to dispel misconceptions related to blood donation and increasing the self-esteem of blood donors by thanking them for their work. Blood donation is said to be a noble and selfless act. A single blood donation can save up to three lives. But a lot of people are hesitant about donating blood due to a variety of reasons including fear of pain, anaemia or needles and their own beliefs and customs. However, blood donation is completely safe for healthy adults.
